# iFoundIt for Schools

**Lost property that finds its way back — without an app, without extra admin.**

Every school loses kit. Coats, bags, equipment, phone pouches — the pile in reception grows every term, and most of it never makes it back to the right pupil. iFoundIt tags turn that around: whoever finds a lost item taps their phone on the tag, and your school gets notified straight away.

## Built for schools, not consumers

Most lost-property tools are built for individuals losing their own keys or wallets. iFoundIt for Schools works differently:

- **Your school is the registered contact** — not the pupil, not a third-party app account.
- **No app for anyone to download.** Any modern smartphone reads the tag by tapping it — that's it.
- **You forward to parents using contact details you already hold.** No new data collection, no new consent process.

---

## Where it's already a fit

- **Phone pouches.** If your school uses locking pouches, a recovery tag means a lost pouch outside school still finds its way home. [Read more →]
- **PE kit and equipment.** Tag it once, recover it fast — less spent replacing kit that was never really lost, just misplaced.
- **Coats, bags, water bottles.** The everyday items that pile up in lost property boxes all year.

---

## Why schools choose iFoundIt

- **No new procurement headache.** Tags add to what you're already buying — pouches, kit, equipment — rather than asking for a new budget line.
- **No pupil data risk.** The school is the contact point throughout. Nothing routes anywhere you don't control.
- **Less replacement spend.** Every item that gets found instead of replaced is money back in your budget.
